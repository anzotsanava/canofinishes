About UsWho We Are

At cano finishes our wood repair services entail meticulous restoration of your furniture. Whether facing scratches, dents, or water damage, our skilled artisans employ a blend of traditional methods and modern techniques to precisely address and revive each piece.

French polishing is renowned for its ability to produce a glass-like surface that not only protects the wood but also highlights its inherent beauty. This technique is often favored for antique furniture restoration, where authenticity and a timeless aesthetic are paramount. While it demands a high level of craftsmanship, the result is a finish that exudes elegance and sophistication, making it a sought-after choice for those who appreciate the artistry and tradition of fine woodworking
