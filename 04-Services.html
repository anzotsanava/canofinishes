What We DoFrench polishing

Repairing wood furniture or structures is often more economical than purchasing new ones. Repairing minor damages or refinishing can give your items a fresh look without the expense of replacement.

Step into the timeless artistry of French polishing, where we delicately apply layers of shellac to create a luminous, durable finish. From vintage dining tables to beloved heirlooms, we preserve and enhance the wood's natural charm, delivering a mirror-like elegance. Complementing this is our modern touch – spray finishing. State-of-the-art equipment and top-quality finishes transform kitchen cabinets, handrails, flooring, High Gloss, marble,stones, doors, and paneling, providing a uniform appearance, enhanced durability, and customizable aesthetics.
